Common questions

Everything you're wondering about.

Does it hurt?+

Most patients are surprised how comfortable it is. We use local anaesthetic and offer sedation, so the procedure itself is painless and recovery is usually mild.

How much do implants cost?+

It depends on how many teeth you're replacing. Your free consultation includes a clear, written quote — and we offer 0% finance to spread the cost.

Am I too old for implants?+

Age is rarely the issue — bone health is what matters, and we assess that during your free scan. We've successfully treated patients well into their 80s.

What happens at the free consultation?+

A 3D scan, a look at your options, and a personalised plan with pricing. No pressure — you decide if and when to move forward.
